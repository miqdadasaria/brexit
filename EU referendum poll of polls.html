UK’s EU referendum

Brexit poll tracker

The UK will hold a referendum on June 23 to decide if it will stay in or leave the European Union. This page summarises the opinion polling on the matter.

Latest developments

Whitehall is preparing for the biggest bureaucratic upheaval for a generation in the event of a Leave vote in tomorrow's historic EU referendum, as the engine of British government is reconfigured.
